# Rezero_Analyitcs
Re:Zero Anime Analytics Dashboard

Project Overview

This project is a fan-made Power BI dashboard that analyzes the anime series Re:Zero − Starting Life in Another World through data visualization and storytelling.

The dataset was manually collected, cleaned, and organized using publicly available information from anime databases. The goal of this project is to transform anime-related data into meaningful insights regarding ratings, story arcs, episode distribution, and character popularity.

Data Sources

Data for this project was collected manually from:

- IMDb
- MyAnimeList (MAL)

Since the anime is still ongoing, some statistics may change as future episodes are released.

Tools Used

- Power BI
- MySQL
- Microsoft Excel

Dataset Information

- Total Episodes Analyzed: 85
- Season 4 (Arc 6) is currently ongoing.
- Approximately 10 episodes are yet to air at the time of creating this dashboard.
- All data collection, cleaning, and organization were performed manually.

Key Insights

Overall Ratings

- The average rating across the series is approximately 8.7/10.

- Arc 6 (Season 4) is currently the highest-rated arc in the series.

- The Priestella Arc is the second-highest-rated arc.

- The highest-rated episode in the dataset is:
  
  Season 4 Episode 8 – Rating: 9.9/10

This episode highlights the strong reception that Season 4 has received from both anime-only viewers and long-time novel readers.

Why Season 4 Is Performing So Well

While support from novel readers and community discussions has contributed to the season's popularity, the success of Season 4 appears to be driven by several factors:

- Strong adaptation of Tappei Nagatsuki's source material.
- High-quality animation and production values.
- Emotional storytelling and character development.
- Consistently positive audience reception.

These factors have helped Arc 6 become one of the most highly praised parts of the franchise.

Character Insights

- The majority of characters in Re:Zero are humans.
- The series also contains a large variety of fantasy races and creatures.
- The Emilia Camp contains the highest number of major characters, largely because the story follows Subaru's involvement with that faction.

Future Popularity Predictions

The following observations are personal predictions based on current fan reception and ongoing Season 4 developments:

- Subaru has a strong chance of becoming the most popular character in the franchise after Season 4 concludes.
- Rem is likely to remain within the Top 10 despite receiving limited screen time.
- Patrasche, Subaru's loyal ground dragon, may enter the Top 3 most beloved characters.
- Shaula has a strong chance of entering the Top 10 rankings.

Anime of the Year Outlook

Based on current ratings, audience reception, production quality, and overall fan engagement, Re:Zero Season 4 has a strong chance of being considered a contender for Anime of the Year if the remaining episodes maintain the same level of quality.

Excluded Analysis

Subaru's death count was intentionally excluded from detailed analysis.

Since Season 4 is still ongoing, any death-related statistics would be incomplete and potentially inaccurate until all episodes have aired.
